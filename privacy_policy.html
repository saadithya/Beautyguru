Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 25, 2025

Introduction

Scholarship ("we", "our", or "us") operates the BeautyGuru – Face Scanner mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the App and outlines your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

Personal Preferences

The App may collect non-identifiable preferences such as:

  • Selected gender for personalized analysis
  • Chosen AI provider

Camera & Image Data

Facial images captured using the camera are:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Used only for facial and skin analysis
  • Not transmitted to our servers
  • Stored locally only if you choose to save results

Usage Data

The App may collect limited diagnostic information (such as app stability data) to improve performance. This data does not include facial images.

How We Use Data

  • To provide facial and skin analysis features
  • To generate personalized recommendations
  • To improve app reliability and performance
  • To troubleshoot technical issues

Third-Party Services

BeautyGuru allows users to connect their own AI API keys (such as OpenAI or Google Gemini). Any data processed using these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Scholarship does not receive or store your API keys or analysis results.

Data Storage & Security

All sensitive data, including images and analysis results, remains on your device. We do not maintain cloud storage or remote databases for personal data.

Data Retention

You have full control over your data. You may delete saved scans and results at any time using your device or by uninstalling the App.

Legal Disclosure

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect legal rights, safety, or security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
