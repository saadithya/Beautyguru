BeautyGuru – Face Scanner

An AI-powered facial analysis app designed to help you understand your skin health, facial symmetry, and appearance — with privacy-first, on-device processing.

About the App

BeautyGuru uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze facial images and provide insights into skin quality, facial balance, and aesthetic attributes. The app is designed for users who want to track their appearance over time and receive personalized skincare guidance.

Key Features

Skin Health Analysis

Analyze hydration levels, texture, pores, acne, blemishes, sun exposure, and overall skin tone consistency.

Facial Symmetry & Structure

Evaluate facial balance, proportions, and alignment to better understand your unique facial features.

Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored skincare and grooming suggestions based on your analysis results.

Progress Tracking

Save scan results locally and compare changes over time to monitor improvements.

Bring Your Own AI API Key

Use your own OpenAI or Google Gemini API key for analysis. You remain in full control of usage and billing.

Permissions & Data Usage

Camera Access

Camera access is required to capture facial images for analysis. Images are processed locally and are not uploaded to external servers.

Local Storage

Storage access is used only to save scan history and results on your device. You can delete this data at any time.

Privacy-First Design

BeautyGuru does not collect, store, or sell personal images or biometric data. All sensitive data stays on your device.

How It Works

  1. Choose your preferred AI provider and add your own API key
  2. Capture three photos: front, left profile, and right profile
  3. AI analyzes facial features and skin condition
  4. View results and personalized recommendations
  5. Track progress using saved scan history
