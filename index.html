BeautyGuru - Face Scanner

Professional AI-powered facial analysis and skincare guidance

About the App

BeautyGuru is an advanced facial analysis application that uses artificial intelligence to provide detailed insights into your skin health, facial symmetry, and overall appearance. Our app helps users understand their facial features and provides personalized skincare recommendations.

Key Features

Skin Health Analysis

Advanced AI analyzes your skin's hydration, texture, pore visibility, acne and blemishes, sun damage, and tone evenness.

Facial Symmetry Assessment

Professional evaluation of facial balance and symmetry to help understand your unique features.

Personalized Recommendations

Get tailored skincare and beauty tips based on your individual facial analysis.

Progress Tracking

Save and compare your scans over time to track improvements in your skin health and appearance.

Multiple AI Providers

Choose between OpenAI and Google Gemini for analysis, ensuring the best results for your needs.

Permissions and Data Usage

Camera Access

The app requires camera access to capture facial images for analysis. Photos are processed locally on your device and are not stored on external servers. Images are used solely for the purpose of facial analysis and are deleted after processing unless you choose to save the scan results.

Storage Access

The app uses device storage to save your scan results and analysis data. This allows you to track your progress over time and review previous scans.

Data Security

All facial images and personal data remain on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your photos to any external servers. Your privacy is our top priority.

How It Works

  1. Complete your profile setup and add your preferred AI API key
  2. Capture three facial photos (front, left, right) for comprehensive analysis
  3. AI processes your images to analyze skin health and facial features
  4. Receive detailed results and personalized recommendations
  5. Track your progress with saved scan history
